What is FadeJunkie?

Not just a brand — a blueprint. Born from real grind, built for students, barbers, and clients who want more from the culture.

Last updated: 2025 • Estimated read time: 4 minutes

I started FadeJunkie after years of cutting without a license — getting good before I got approved. I built a name the long way. When I finally made it official, I saw the system didn’t support us — not really. Students were lost, licensed barbers were isolated, and clients didn’t know where to look.

FadeJunkie is what I wish existed when I was coming up. It’s the blueprint I had to figure out the hard way.

FadeJunkie is a lane for three types of people:

  • Students – grinding toward that license with study tools and prep resources that actually help.
  • Barbers – already licensed, looking to grow their presence, connect, and sharpen up.
  • Clients – folks who want real fades, real talent, and no BS.

It’s more than a directory.

It’s a full system: eBooks, flashcards, test prep tools, brand kits, booking links, mentorship — all organized by what stage you're at.

Built by barbers. Run by the culture.

No investors. No tech bros. Just a network built from the ground up, with tools made by someone who’s lived the hustle. This is for us.
